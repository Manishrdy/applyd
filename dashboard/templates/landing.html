{# ===== FAQ ======================================================== #}

Common questions

Asked & answered.

Where do these jobs come from?
Postings are scraped directly from each company's applicant tracking system — Greenhouse, Lever, Ashby, Workday, and 43 others. The corpus is the open jobhive dataset (MIT-licensed). No re-broadcasting, no ad inventory.
How fresh are the postings?
A full ingest cycle runs every 24 hours, and the rolling window shows the last 30 days. Each row carries an age stamp so you can filter to anything indexed in the last hour, day, or week. Note: iCIMS sometimes exposes an expected start date as the posting date, so 24-hour freshness cannot be guaranteed for iCIMS postings.
Is applyd free?
Yes — free during preview, no credit card required. Pricing for the auto-apply agent will be announced before it ships, and the search/save layer stays free.
What happens with my data?
Your account stores an email, a salted PBKDF2-SHA256 hash of your password, and your saved-job list. We don't sell resumes and we don't ship third-party trackers. Sessions auto-expire after 14 days.
When does the auto-apply agent ship?
The agent is in private preview with a small cohort while we tune the audit trail and approval flow. Sign up now to claim a seat — your saved shortlist becomes its first work queue.
